SEMAA Marine Safety Policy v1.0

Organization: SEMAA Marine Operations
Effective Date: 2026-01-15
Owner: Marine Compliance Team

Section 1. Vessel Classes
- Class A vessels: coastal survey boats under 40 meters.
- Class B vessels: offshore support vessels over 40 meters.

Section 2. Inspection Cadence
- Class A vessels must complete a full inspection every 30 days.
- Class B vessels must complete a full inspection every 14 days.
- Emergency inspection is mandatory within 24 hours after any engine overheating event.

Section 3. Critical Contacts
- Safety Director: Alice Chen
- Fleet Manager: Brian Wu
- Incident Hotline: +886-2-5555-1200

Section 4. Fuel Handling
- Diesel transfer requires two-person confirmation.
- Maximum transfer window is 90 minutes.
- Any transfer over 70 minutes must be annotated in transfer log.

Section 5. Non-Compliance Actions
- First violation: written warning and corrective training within 7 days.
- Second violation: temporary vessel suspension up to 72 hours.
- Third violation: escalation to executive compliance board.
