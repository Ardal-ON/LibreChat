SEMAA Budget and Procurement Plan BP-2026-H1

Department: Marine Operations and Safety
Prepared By: Finance Lead Irene Kuo
Period: 2026-01-01 to 2026-06-30

Budget Allocation
- Safety training budget: USD 120,000.
- Spare parts budget: USD 340,000.
- Emergency response reserve: USD 80,000.

Procurement Priorities
- Priority 1: Cooling pump filters CPF-9, target 1,200 units.
- Priority 2: Relay clamps RC-12, target 300 units.
- Priority 3: Lubricant LT-55, target 1,500 liters.

Vendor Strategy
- Primary vendor for CPF-9: OceanCore Parts Ltd.
- Secondary vendor for CPF-9: HarborTech Supply Co.
- Dual-vendor policy required for all critical engine components.

Controls and Thresholds
- Any single PO above USD 50,000 requires dual approval.
- Emergency reserve usage above USD 20,000 requires Safety Director notification within 12 hours.
- Monthly variance above 8 percent triggers executive review.

Milestones
- March checkpoint: complete 45 percent of CPF-9 annual target.
- June checkpoint: complete 70 percent of CPF-9 annual target.
